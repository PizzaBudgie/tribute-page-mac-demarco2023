Mac DeMarco

A multi-talented musician and songwriter

Here's a brief overview of Mac DeMarco's life and career:

  • 1990 - Born in Duncan, British Columbia, Canada
  • 2008 - Starts performing music in various bands and releases his first solo EP "Heat Wave"
  • 2012 - Releases his debut full-length album "2", which garners critical acclaim and begins to establish his signature sound
  • 2014 - Releases "Salad Days", which becomes his most successful album to date, and embarks on a world tour
  • 2017 - Releases "This Old Dog", which showcases a more mature and introspective side of his songwriting
  • 2021 - Releases "Here Comes the Cowboy Demos", a collection of demo recordings from his 2019 album "Here Comes the Cowboy"
  • 2023 - Releases "One Wayne G", an insanely huge collection of recordings and its 9hr and 30 mins long!

"I don’t think I’m particularly talented at anything – maybe barbecuing. But it’s definitely something that I’ve worked at and got lucky with."

-- Mac DeMarco on his success in music
